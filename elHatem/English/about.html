About Us

Why choose Al Hatem ?

Because we care about building trust and strengthening partnership. We are committed to the finest work methods to preserve the environment and the health and safety of workers. We always strive to achieve customer satisfaction by adhering to the highest quality standards and providing outstanding customer service and Raising efficiency, improving productivity and continuous development .

Our Vision

To be one of the pioneer manufacturers in Kuwait in the field of manufacturing and processing of high quality clothes for military and trading purposes. We are interested to exist extensively in both local and GCC markets.

Our Mission

Presenting high quality products for our valuable customers and achieving our partner's interests through focusing on innovation, product quality customer service while developing performance.

Stablished Sinced 2005

Equipment and supplies for the military forces in various departments of the state of Kuwait are considered important elements for those who work in these vital departments. This is the same for the requirements of the local and external markets for the various service and industrial sectors such as the ministries and governmental departments. To ensure the highest quality for such requirements and supplies for both civil and military sectors as per the best International standard

Al-Hatem Manufacturing Company has been established for producing military and ready-made clothes to meet such requirements
